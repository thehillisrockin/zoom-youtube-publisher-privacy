Privacy Policy — Zoom YouTube Publisher

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Zoom YouTube Publisher ("the App") is a private, single-operator automation built and used by Phil Stringer (Stratus Global LLC) to publish his own live training and workshop recordings to his own YouTube channel. The App is not offered to the public and does not create accounts for, or process data belonging to, any third-party users.

What the App does

When the operator starts it, the App retrieves a recording the operator made in their own Zoom account, performs simple automated editing, and uploads the finished video to the operator's own YouTube channel as an unlisted video. It runs on the operator's own computer.

Information the App accesses

  • Google / YouTube data: Through the YouTube Data API, the App uploads videos to, and manages videos on, the operator's own YouTube channel. It accesses only the operator's own Google account and does not read, collect, or store other users' YouTube data.
  • Zoom data: The App accesses the operator's own Zoom cloud recordings in order to download the file to be published.
  • Credentials: OAuth tokens for the operator's own Google and Zoom accounts are stored locally on the operator's computer so the App can run without re-authenticating each time.

How information is used

Information is used solely to perform the action the operator requested: editing the operator's own recording and uploading it to the operator's own YouTube channel. The App does not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to that function.

Sharing and transfer

The App does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data flows only between the operator's own Zoom account, the operator's own computer, and the operator's own YouTube channel.

Google API Services Limited Use disclosure

Zoom YouTube Publisher's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data retention

Locally stored credentials remain on the operator's computer until the operator deletes them or revokes access in their Google or Zoom account settings. Downloaded recordings are handled on the operator's own machine and are not retained by any external service operated by the App.

Your choices

The operator can revoke the App's access at any time via their Google Account permissions page or their Zoom account's app settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to admin@philstringer.com.

Operated by Phil Stringer (Stratus Global LLC). This policy applies to the Zoom YouTube Publisher automation only.
